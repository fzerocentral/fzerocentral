Welcome to F-Zero Central! We are a competition and fan community for the high-speed, futuristic racing game series known as F-Zero. Our site features Time Attack ladders for each of the 6 F-Zero games released on console.

We are proud to represent F-Zero pilots of all skill levels here, from world record holders to those completely new to the series. At our forums, we welcome competitive discussion as well as general chat about F-Zero. Register an account to join us in the ladders and forums. You can also find a lot of our members at the F-Zero Nexus Discord chat.

F-Zero Central is one of the oldest speedrun or time attack communities on the Internet, with over 15 years of history. Learn more about us here. If you need any assistance with the site, feel free to ask us on the forums, Facebook, or Twitter.
