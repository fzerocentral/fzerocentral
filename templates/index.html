Welcome to F-Zero Central! We are a competition and fan community for the high-speed, futuristic racing game series known as F-Zero. Our site features Time Attack ladders for each of the 6 F-Zero games released on console.

We are proud to represent F-Zero pilots of all skill levels here, from world record holders to those completely new to the series. You can find discussion about the website on our Discord server. You can also find a lot of our members at the F-Zero Nexus Discord server, which welcomes competitive discussion as well as general chat about F-Zero.

F-Zero Central is one of the oldest speedrun or time attack communities on the Internet, with over 15 years of history. Recently we've been short-handed in terms of site development, so some site features may be lacking for a while, but we'll do our best to keep things running. If you need any assistance with the site, feel free to ask us on our Discord, Facebook, or Twitter.
